1. The "Big 6" Art Action Verbs (Imperatives)
Context: These cover 90% of the activities mentioned in the PDF (Saber Hacer).
Verb	Spanish	Context / Phrase
Draw	Dibujar	"Draw a circle." / "Draw a line."
Color	Colorear	"Color the picture."
Paint	Pintar	"Paint the background."
Cut	Cortar	"Cut the paper." / "Cut carefully."
Glue	Pegar	"Glue the shape." / "Use the glue stick."
Wash	Lavar	"Wash your brush." / "Wash your hands."
Trainer Tip: Practice these with TPR (Total Physical Response). You say "Cut," she mimics cutting immediately.

2. The "Remote Control" (Classroom Management)
Context: She needs to control 30 kids in a practical setting. These are short, sharp commands to maintain authority.
Getting Attention:
•	"Class, class!" (She says this) -> "Yes, yes!" (Teach her to train the students to reply. It saves her voice).
•	"Eyes on me." (Point to eyes, point to chest).
•	"Listen to me." (Point to ears).
•	"Silence, please." (Finger on lips).
•	"Freeze!" (Essential for the Dance/Theater units in Grades 2, 3, 5).
Movement & logistics:
•	"Stand up."
•	"Sit down."
•	"Line up." (At the door).
•	"Come here." (To the carpet/front).
•	"Go back to your place."

3. The Entry Routine (The First 5 Minutes)
Context: Establishing the "English Environment" immediately.
Greetings:
•	"Good morning, everyone." / "Good afternoon."
•	"Hello!" / "Welcome."
•	"How are you today?" (Expect: "Fine, thank you").
Preparation (Based on "Recursos" & Safety):
•	"Put on your apron." (Crucial for Grade 1-3 painting).
•	"Take out your pencil case."
•	"Clear your desk." (Only art supplies allowed).
•	"No water bottles." (Safety with electronics/paint).

4. Technology & Demonstration (The "iPad" Phrases)
Context: She relies on her iPad/Projector. This allows her to teach visually without explaining complex concepts in English.
Phrase	Context
"Look at the screen."	Directing attention to the projection.
"Watch the video."	Before playing a clip.
"Look at this picture."	Showing an example.
"Like this."	The most important phrase. She demonstrates a physical action (e.g., mixing paint) and says "Like this."
"Copy me."	For dance/theater warm-ups.
"One moment, please."	If technical issues happen.

6. The Exit Routine (Clean Up)
Context: Leaving the room clean is part of the "Saber Ser/Convivir" competencies.
•	"Stop!" (Signal end of activity).
•	"Clean up." (General command).
•	"Cap the glue." / "Cap the markers." (Prevent drying out).
•	"Throw it in the bin." (Trash).
•	"Wash your brushes."
•	"Dry your table."
•	"Goodbye! See you next class."
