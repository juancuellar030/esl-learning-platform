**1. Step-by-Step Connectors (Sequencers)**

_Use these words to organize the instructions during a demonstration (e.g., how to make a puppet or
mix colors)._

```
Connector Spanish Equivalent Sentence in Context
First Primero First , draw a big circle on the paper.
Second Segundo Second , cut out the circle carefully.
Next Siguiente / Luego Next , take your glue stick.
Then Entonces / Después Then , stick the eyes onto the face.
After that Después de eso After that , wait for the glue to dry.
Finally / Lastly Finalmente / Por último Finally , write your name on the back.
Now Ahora Now , show me your work.
```
**2. Discourse Markers & Transition Words**

_Use these to manage the flow of the class, grab attention, or change the subject._

```
Discourse Marker Spanish Equivalent Context / Usage
OK / Alright Bueno / Listo / Bien OK , let's start the class. / Alright , eyes on me.
So... Entonces... / Así que... The paint is wet, so be careful.
But Pero Use the scissors, but don't run.
And Y Take a pencil and an eraser.
Also También / Además Also , remember to wash your brushes.
For example Por ejemplo Draw a fruit, for example , an apple or a banana.
Ready? ¿Listos? Ready? Let's go! (Great for Dance/Music starts).
Remember Recuerden Remember , do not use too much glue.
Look Miren Look , this is my puppet.
Like this Así / Como esto Move your hands like this.
Wait Esperen Wait a minute, please. Don't start yet.
Excuse me Permiso / Disculpe Excuse me , please sit down.
Very important Muy importante Very important : put your name on the paper.
```
