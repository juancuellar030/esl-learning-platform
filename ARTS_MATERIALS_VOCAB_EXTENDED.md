Here is the vocabulary list categorized by use (Art Supplies, Crafts/Recycled Materials, Theater/Performance, and Classroom Objects) to help you structure the review lesson logically.

1. Basic Art Supplies (Drawing & Painting)
Word	Spanish Equivalent	Sentence in Context
Acrylics / Vinyl paint	Vinilos	Pour a little bit of vinyl paint on the palette.
Brush / Paintbrush	Pincel	Wash your brush before changing colors.
Canvas	Lienzo	We usually paint on paper, not on canvas.
Charcoal	Carboncillo	Use charcoal to make the dark shadows.
Colored pencils	Colores (lápices de color)	Sharpen your colored pencils, please.
Crayons	Crayolas	Press hard with the crayons to make the color bright.
Eraser	Borrador	Use your eraser to fix the mistake.
Grid	Cuadrícula	Draw the image using the grid as a guide.
Markers	Marcadores	Outline your drawing with a black marker.
Palette	Paleta (para mezclar)	Mix the yellow and blue on your palette.
Paper	Papel	Take one sheet of paper from the pile.
Pencil	Lápiz	Sketch lightly with your pencil first.
Sharpener	Sacapuntas	Use the bin when you use your sharpener.
Sketchbook	Cuaderno de dibujo / Block	Open your sketchbook to a new page.
Tempera / Poster paint	Témpera	Be careful not to spill the tempera.
Watercolors	Acuarelas	You need water to activate the watercolors.

Word	Spanish Equivalent	Sentence in Context

Cardstock	Cartulina	Cardstock is stronger than normal paper.
Clay / Playdough	Arcilla / Plastilina	Roll the clay into a ball.
Fabric / Cloth	Tela	Cut a piece of fabric for the puppet's dress.
Glitter	Escarcha / Brillantina	Sprinkle some glitter on the wet glue.
Glue (liquid)	Colbón / Pegante líquido	You only need a drop of liquid glue.
Glue stick	Pegante en barra	Use the glue stick for the paper shapes.
Magazine	Revista	Cut pictures from the old magazine for the collage.
Newspaper	Periódico	Cover the desk with newspaper to keep it clean.
Plastic bottle	Botella plástica	We will recycle this plastic bottle to make a flower.
Popsicle stick	Palito de paleta	Glue the character onto the popsicle stick.
Recyclables	Material reciclable	Bring clean recyclables from home tomorrow.
Scissors	Tijeras	Hold the scissors by the handle, please.
Tape	Cinta adhesiva	Use tape to stick the back of the mask.
Wool / Yarn	Lana	Use wool to make the hair for the puppet.

Word	Spanish Equivalent	Sentence in Context
